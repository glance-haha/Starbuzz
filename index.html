The world beverage industry has reached a considerable scale of production since the beginning of the 20th century. Mineral water, carbonated beverages, fruit juices, vegetable juices, milk, beer and wine have all formed large-scale and automated production systems.

There are many varieties of beverages, which are divided into two categories according to the production process: alcoholic beverages and non-alcoholic beverages. Alcoholic beverages are made from sorghum, barley, rice or fruits, fermented or distilled, including various wines and blended wines.
